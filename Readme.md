2. Stretch Routine Content (The Output)
   When the user clicks "Generate Routine," your app will need to display text based on what they selected. Here is professional, ergonomic content for the 2-minute variations of each target area.

Option 1: Digital Eye Strain (2 Min)

Title: The 20-20-20 Refresh + Palming

Instructions:

Look away from your screen and focus on an object roughly 20 feet away for 20 seconds.

Blink slowly and deeply 10 times to rehydrate your eyes.

Rub your palms together briskly until they feel warm. Gently cup your warm hands over your closed eyes (without pressing on the eyeballs) and rest in the darkness for the remaining time.

Option 2: Neck & Shoulders (2 Min)

Title: Seated Neck Release & Shoulder Rolls

Instructions:

Sit up straight. Gently drop your right ear toward your right shoulder until you feel a stretch on the left side of your neck. Hold for 30 seconds.

Slowly return to the center, then drop your left ear to your left shoulder. Hold for 30 seconds.

For the remaining minute, slowly roll your shoulders backward in large, smooth circles.

Option 3: Wrists & Hands (2 Min)

Title: Prayer Stretch & Extensor Flex

Instructions:

Place your palms together in a "prayer" position in front of your chest. Slowly lower your hands toward your lap while keeping your palms pressed together until you feel a stretch in your forearms. Hold for 30 seconds.

Extend your right arm straight in front of you with your palm facing down. Use your left hand to gently press your right fingers down toward the floor. Hold for 30 seconds. Swap arms and repeat.

Option 4: Lower Back (2 Min)

Title: Seated Spinal Twist

Instructions:

Sit up tall with your feet flat on the floor.

Cross your right leg over your left leg. Place your left hand on the outside of your right knee, and your right hand on the back of your chair.

Gently twist your torso to the right, looking over your right shoulder. Hold for 30 seconds.

Slowly untwist, cross your left leg over your right, and repeat the twist on the opposite side.
